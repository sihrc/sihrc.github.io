User Visits

Our team conducted a series of 6 user visits of different chefs or people who are mostly in charge of food within their household. We interviewed:
  • A young married couple with no kids
  • A stay-at-home mom with 4 kids
  • The mother and father of 2 teenage girls
  • The mother of two elementary school kids
  • The mother and father of two younger children
  • A mother of kids who are no longer at home
